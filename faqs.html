Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the size of the space and how many people can it hold?

The Golden 7 Venue is approximately 1,250 square feet and can hold about 65-75 people comfortably.

2. How can I schedule a viewing?

We host open houses 2 times per week (DATES TBD).Feel free to stop by our space at any of those times for a viewing. Please be sure to give us a call beforehand to ensure that a member from our team is there to walk you through the space.

3. How far in advance should I book an event?

We recommend booking your event at least 4 weeks in advance to ensure your desired date is available. If you need to book closer to your date, please reach out to us so that we can try our hardest to accommodate your needs.

4. How much do you hold as a deposit?

$150 is due upon booking a date and goes toward your total balance. This $150 is to hold your event date. Please kindly note that this deposit in non-refundable. There is also a $150 security deposit fee which is refunded at the end of your event *contingent upon successful compliance with contract terms

5. When is the rest of my balance due?

The remaining balance is due 14 days prior to your event. If you are scheduling an event 2 weeks prior to the event date please kindly note that your full balance is due at time of booking. Please kindly note that any balance not fully paid within 14 days may result in event cancellation and $150 deposit not being refunded.

6. What is provided with my event?

-8 6-foot rectangular tables -52 Metal Chairs -3 55” TVs for images and/or streaming -Bluetooth Speaker for music - High Speed WiFi *Popular add-ons include 32” high-top tables and greenery backdrop for photos.

7. Can I bring my own food and alcohol?

Absolutely! Please keep in mind that while alcohol is permitted, you cannot sell alcohol at our location during your event

8. Is there a parking lot?

While there is no parking lot, there is an ample amount of free street parking, especially after 6pm.

9. Are set-up and breakdown times included in my rental time?

Set-up and breakdown time is included in your rental period. Additional time can be added.

10. What time slots are able to be booked?

Saturdays: 12pm - 5pm // 7pm - 12am Sundays: 12pm - 5pm // 7pm - 12am

11. What type of events can I host?

While these are just some examples, please keep in mind that we provide space for a plethora of events. Events include birthday parties, baby showers, pop-up shops, art shows, fashion shows, fundraisers, book signings, photo shoots, social meetups, religious gatherings, fitness classes, educational courses, weddings, etc.
